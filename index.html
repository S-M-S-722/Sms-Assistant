CAREER CHAPTERS

CHAPTER I: THE BARCELONA ERA

17 seasons of dominance. From 2004 to 2021, Messi redefined football at FC Barcelona, winning 10 La Liga titles and 4 Champions Leagues, scoring 672 goals for a single club—a record that may never be broken.

CHAPTER II: ETERNAL GLORY

The quest for international gold. In 2021, Messi broke the drought with the Copa América. In 2022, he completed football by winning the FIFA World Cup in Qatar, followed by a second consecutive Copa América in 2024.

CHAPTER III: THE AMERICAN DYNASTY

As of 2026, Messi has transformed Inter Miami into a global powerhouse. After winning the 2024 Supporters' Shield and the 2025 MLS Cup, he stands as the most decorated player in the history of the sport.
